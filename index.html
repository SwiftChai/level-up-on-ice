About the Seminar

Master the Fundamentals,
Embrace the Challenge

Level Up on Ice welcomes skaters of all levels! Work in small groups with experienced coaches who will help you master the fundamentals tailored to your skill level. Sessions dive deep into spins, jumps, edges, stroking, and balance — every skater leaves with new skills, knowledge, and confidence.

🏅

Expert Coaching

Work closely with experienced coaches in small groups to refine your technique and elevate your performance.

🧠

Holistic Approach

Gain insights from guest speakers on sports psychology, nutrition, and off-ice conditioning.

⛸️

Skill-Focused Sessions

Specialized sessions on spins, jumps, edges, stroking, and balance for every skill level.
