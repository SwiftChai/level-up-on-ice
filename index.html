Next-Level Skating Starts Here

Master the Fundamentals, Embrace the Challenge

Level Up on Ice welcomes skaters of all levels! Work in small groups with experienced coaches who will help you master the fundamentals tailored to your skill level. This isn't just about what happens on the ice—you'll also benefit from guest motivational speakers and specialized classes covering sports psychology, diet and nutrition, and off-ice conditioning. Sessions will dive deep into spins, jumps, edges, stroking, and balance, ensuring that every skater—beginner to advanced—leaves with new skills, knowledge, and confidence!

What to Expect

Skate to Your Full Potential

  • Expert Coaching: Work closely with experienced coaches in small groups to refine your skills.
  • Holistic Approach: Gain insights from guest speakers on sports psychology, nutrition, and off-ice conditioning.
  • Skill-Focused Sessions: Engage in specialized sessions on spins, jumps, edges, stroking, and balance.
